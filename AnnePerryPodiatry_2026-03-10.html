What We Treat

Expert Foot Care, From Routine to Specialist

From everyday chiropody to specialist nail surgery, our Warrington podiatry clinic handles a full range of foot and lower-limb conditions.

✂️

General Chiropody

Comprehensive foot health appointments covering nail trimming, hard skin care, and routine maintenance, keeping your feet in peak condition all year round.

Includes nail cutting and filing, hard skin and callus management, assessment of nail health, and personalised advice on home foot care. Recommended every 6–8 weeks for optimal foot health.
💅

Ingrown Toenail Treatment

Fast, effective treatment for painful ingrown toenails. Minor nail surgery performed under local anaesthetic where needed, providing lasting relief after just one visit.

We offer both conservative treatment (careful nail edge removal) and partial nail avulsion surgery under local anaesthetic. The procedure is quick, highly effective, and most patients are walking comfortably the same day.
🩹

Verruca Treatment

Evidence-based treatments, including topical acids, cryotherapy, and needling, that tackle stubborn plantar warts at the root when home remedies have failed.

We assess each verruca individually and recommend the most appropriate treatment. Cryotherapy and needling have excellent success rates even for longstanding verrucas. Multiple treatment options mean we can find what works for you.
🪨

Corn & Callus Removal

Skilled, pain-free debridement of corns and hard skin calluses, providing immediate comfort and practical advice to prevent them from returning.

Corns and calluses are removed using specialist instruments for instant relief. We also identify underlying causes, such as pressure points or footwear issues, and advise on prevention to reduce recurrence.
🦶

Biomechanics & Orthotics

Detailed gait analysis to identify movement irregularities causing foot, knee, or lower-back pain, with custom orthotic insoles prescribed where appropriate.

Biomechanical assessment involves observing how you walk and stand, identifying any abnormal forces on the feet and lower limbs. Custom orthotics are prescribed where needed, providing targeted support to correct alignment and relieve pain.
✨

Wilde-Pedique Nail Reconstruction

Cosmetic nail reconstruction for thickened, damaged, or missing toenails, restoring a natural, healthy appearance so you feel confident going barefoot again.

Using the Wilde-Pedique system, we build up a realistic-looking nail that is indistinguishable from a natural nail. Ideal for damaged, fungal, or post-surgery nails. The result is immediate and can last several months.

Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Answers to the questions our Warrington patients ask us most often.

What does a podiatry appointment involve? +

Your appointment begins with a brief consultation to understand your foot health history and any concerns you have. Your podiatrist will then examine your feet, nails, and skin thoroughly. Treatment may include nail care, skin care, verruca treatment, or footwear advice. Most appointments take 30–45 minutes.

How do I treat an ingrown toenail in Warrington? +

Don't attempt to cut or dig out an ingrown toenail at home, as this can cause infection. Call us on 01925 244666 for a prompt appointment. We offer conservative treatment and minor nail surgery under local anaesthetic for persistent cases. Most patients feel immediate relief after their first appointment.

What is the best verruca treatment available in Warrington? +

The most effective verruca treatment depends on the size, location, and duration of the verruca. At our clinic, we use a range of evidence-based treatments including topical acids, cryotherapy (freezing), and needling. Your podiatrist will recommend the most appropriate option during your assessment.

Do you treat children and elderly patients? +

Absolutely. We see patients of all ages. Children with verrucas, ingrown nails, or gait concerns are very welcome, and we take extra care to ensure they feel comfortable. We also offer home visit appointments for elderly or less mobile patients who find travelling to the clinic difficult.

Do you offer home visits in Warrington? +

Yes, we offer home visit appointments for patients who are unable to travel to our Padgate Lane clinic due to mobility issues, illness, or other circumstances. Please call us on 01925 244666 to discuss your requirements and we'll arrange a convenient time.

What are your opening hours? +

We are open Monday to Saturday 9:00am–5:00pm and Sunday 10:00am–4:00pm. We also offer evening appointments for patients who cannot attend during standard daytime hours. Call us on 01925 244666 to check availability and book.

Is podiatry the same as chiropody? +

Yes, podiatry and chiropody are the same profession. "Chiropody" is the traditional British term, while "podiatry" is the modern, internationally recognised equivalent. All practitioners at our clinic are fully qualified and HCPC-registered.
